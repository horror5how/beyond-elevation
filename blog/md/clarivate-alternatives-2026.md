# The Best Clarivate Alternatives in 2026: Unbundling the IP Giant

Clarivate is not one product. It is a holding company for IP tools: Derwent for patent data, IPfolio and FoundationIP for management, CompuMark for trademark watching, Darts-ip for litigation data. The bundle is the point, and the bundle is why the invoice is large. The best Clarivate alternative is usually unbundling: keep the piece you use, replace the rest.

## Why Teams Leave Clarivate

Bundled contracts hide per-product cost, renewals arrive with increases, and mid-size companies end up funding enterprise infrastructure. Post-acquisition support quality across its product lines is a recurring complaint in user communities.

## The Best Clarivate Alternatives by Product Line

### Replacing Derwent (patent search and analytics)

Questel Orbit, PatBase, PatSnap, or free coverage via Lens.org. Full comparison in our [Derwent alternatives guide](/blog/posts/derwent-innovation-alternatives-2026/).

### Replacing IPfolio / FoundationIP (IP management)

Questel Equinox for mid-market, Anaqua for enterprise parity, Alt Legal for trademark-led dockets. Reverse view in the [Anaqua alternatives guide](/blog/posts/anaqua-alternatives-2026/).

### Replacing CompuMark (trademark watch)

Corsearch is the head-to-head competitor. For lighter needs, Markify and TrademarkNow-class tools watch marks at a fraction of the cost.

### Replacing Darts-ip (litigation analytics)

Lex Machina and Docket Navigator for US litigation data; Darts-ip remains strongest for multi-jurisdiction coverage, so negotiate rather than replace if global disputes matter to you.

### Replacing all of it with analysis

If the entire Clarivate stack feeds a quarterly strategy conversation, buy the conversation. A [fractional IP strategist](/blog/posts/fractional-patent-counsel-vs-patsnap/) working from free and mid-tier databases delivers the decisions the dashboards were supposed to enable.

## How to Choose

List every Clarivate product on your invoice, mark the ones opened in the last quarter, and get standalone quotes for only those. Most teams discover they use one product out of four. Unbundling typically cuts the spend in half even when the replacement is another paid tool.

## FAQ

### How much does Clarivate cost?

Pricing is contract-only and bundle-dependent. Enterprise IP bundles are reported in the high five to six figures annually. Single products negotiated standalone come in far lower, which is exactly why bundles are pushed.

### Who competes with Clarivate overall?

Questel is the closest like-for-like across search and management. PatSnap competes in analytics, Anaqua in management, Corsearch in trademarks. Nobody matches the full bundle, which is fine, because almost nobody needs the full bundle.

### Is Clarivate the same as Derwent?

Derwent is one Clarivate product line, the patent data and search side. Clarivate acquired it in 2016 along with many other IP tools.
