Founders ask me what changes in the first 30 days. Here is the actual map.

Week 1. I sit with you and your two senior leads. Three sessions. We map every patent, dataset, trade secret, and unique process you own onto one page. Most founders cannot do this from memory. Almost no one has it written down.

Week 2. I audit your top 20 vendor contracts and your AI provider terms. I flag every clause that lets a third party train on, retain, or replicate your output. Founders are usually shocked at what is in there.

Week 3. I cluster your IP into commercial groups and map each cluster to two or three non-competing industries. We pick one cluster and one industry. That is the first licensing target.

Week 4. We draft the licensing pitch. Pricing tiers, scope, exclusivity. Then we approach one target company. Not five. One. Test the offer. Refine. Repeat.

By day 30 you have a one-page IP map, a clean contract risk register, and a live licensing conversation. That is the leverage compounding from month two onward.
