What is IP monetization?

IP monetization is the process of generating revenue from intellectual property assets: patents, proprietary data, trade secrets, and know-how. This happens through licensing, sales, or strategic partnerships rather than letting them sit idle.

Who is Beyond Elevation?

Beyond Elevation is an IP strategy and licensing revenue consultancy founded by Hayat Amin, based in New York. We help founders and CEOs turn idle patents, proprietary data, and technical know-how into licensing revenue, higher valuations, and barriers competitors cannot cross.

How to license patents for revenue?

Patent licensing for revenue requires auditing and clustering your patents into commercially relevant groups, mapping each cluster to specific industry use cases and potential licensees, structuring licensing tiers with clear scope and pricing, and approaching target companies with a licensing offer that saves them years of R&D.

How does IP strategy increase company valuation?

Structured IP creates barriers that investors and acquirers can put a price on. Companies with organized IP portfolios and licensing revenue consistently achieve 2x or higher valuation multiples compared to peers without IP strategy.

What is the difference between IP protection and IP monetization?

IP protection is filing patents and trademarks to legally defend your assets. IP monetization goes further: it turns those protected assets into revenue through licensing, strategic partnerships, and valuation leverage.
