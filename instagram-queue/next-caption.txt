Most people don't quit jobs.

They quit the feeling that their work doesn't matter.

4 signs your work has lost its meaning:

→ The wins feel invisible — you do good work, nobody sees it
→ The role becomes admin, not impact — 70% process, 30% of what you're actually good at
→ The expertise sits unused — you have answers nobody asks for
→ Success arrives but Sunday nights still feel heavy

AI isn't creating this problem. It's just exposing work that was never truly human to begin with.

The question isn't whether the world is changing. It's whether your work will survive the filter.
