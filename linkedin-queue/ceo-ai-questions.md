# CEO / SME-owner AI questions — content line for LinkedIn AI posts (3-5)

Source: live research (last30days engine: Reddit r/Entrepreneur, r/smallbusiness, r/AI_Agents, r/startups + HN + YouTube) and web
(SBE Council, US Chamber, SBA/SCORE, McKinsey 3.7x ROI, Business.com 2026 SMB AI Outlook). Refreshed 2026-07-16.

These are the ACTUAL things small-to-mid business CEOs search and ask about using AI to make their company
better, faster, more efficient, and to grow revenue. Each AI post (slots 3-5) answers ONE of these, a DIFFERENT
one every time. Rotate the operations function (sales / support / marketing / finance / hiring / supply chain / CS / analytics / strategy).

## Efficiency & operations
1. Which parts of my business should I hand to AI first?
2. How do I cut admin hours without hiring anyone?
3. How do I actually know if an AI tool is working, not just busy?
4. What is the fastest AI win I can ship this month with no engineer?
5. How do I use AI to kill the meeting-and-email time drain?
6. How do I audit where AI would save the most money across my ops?
7. How do I build an AI SOP so the output is consistent every time?

## Revenue & sales
8. How do I use AI to find and score leads worth chasing?
9. How can AI shorten my sales cycle?
10. Can a business my size run AI dynamic pricing?
11. How do I use AI to follow up with every lead 24/7?
12. How do I forecast revenue with AI instead of guessing?
13. How do I use AI to upsell and expand existing customers?

## Customer & support
14. How do I use AI for support without it sounding like a bot?
15. How do I personalise the customer experience with AI at small scale?
16. How do I use AI to cut churn before it happens?

## Marketing & content
17. How do I use AI for content without it reading generic?
18. How do I get found in AI answers now that search is changing?
19. How do I run better ads with AI on a small budget?

## Finance & back office

21. How do I use AI to speed up invoicing and collections?
22. Fractional CFO or an AI finance tool — which do I need first?

## People & hiring

24. How do I upskill my team on AI without a big training budget?

## Strategy, risk & getting started
26. How do I get my whole team using AI, not just one power user?

29. What AI mistakes are quietly hurting small businesses right now?

32. How do I tell a real AI vendor from hype?
33. How do autonomous AI agents actually work for a business like mine?
