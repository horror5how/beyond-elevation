No floor clause means your licence is hope.

A licensee can sit on your patent and pay nothing. You gave away exclusivity and received zero in return.

Three clauses every licence needs:
1. Annual minimum guarantee. a fixed floor payment regardless of sales volume
2. Audit rights. the right to inspect the licensee's books once per year
3. Reversion trigger. exclusivity reverts if the minimum is not met within 90 days

Royalty revenue with a floor converts at an 8x revenue multiple at exit.

↳ Founders: does your current licence have a minimum guarantee or a hope?

$2.3M product launch. Stopped by an injunction nobody ran.

Freedom to Operate is not a legal formality. It is the one check that tells you whether your product can ship without a competitor pulling the hand brake.

Three FTO layers every founder needs:
1. Claim-level search. map your product features against active claims, not just titles
2. Jurisdiction scope. US filing alone misses 40% of relevant patent families
3. Design-around options. document three alternatives before you ship, not after

73% of acquirers find at least one IP defect before signing the LOI. FTO is how you remove it first.
