Your AI model depreciates 40% in 12 months.

Your licensing deal probably does not account for that.

Most AI licensing contracts have no performance benchmark clause. No refresh schedule. No royalty step-down.

When the model degrades, licensees stop paying. You hold a depreciating asset with no contract protection.

3 clauses fix this: performance floor, model refresh schedule, royalty step-down trigger.

Miss any one and your recurring revenue becomes a one-off sale.
