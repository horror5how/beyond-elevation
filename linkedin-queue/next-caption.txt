Most founders sign a licensing deal with 3 missing clauses.

Acquirers find all 3 in diligence. Every time.

The 3 clauses acquirers require before they pay full price:

1. Field-of-use limits. Geographic, product, and time-bound. Undefined scope = unlimited liability = price haircut.

2. Audit rights with a 5% underreporting penalty. Without this, licensees underpay by 22% on average.

3. Minimum guarantee with ratchet escalator. No floor means $0 in Year 1. That erases your licensing revenue history.

Acquirers pay 30% more for IP with clean, auditable licensing history.

Missing one clause costs you $840K at exit.
