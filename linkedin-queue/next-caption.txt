Fintech founders think you can't patent financial methods.

That assumption costs $6M at Series B.

6 layers of IP exist in every fintech. Most protect 1.

1. Core algorithm patents — fraud scoring, payment routing, risk models (§101-eligible with the right claim architecture)

2. Compliance trade secrets — your KYC/AML workflow is worth more sealed than published

3. Risk model IP — the scoring method, training pipeline, feature engineering

4. Open banking architecture — API orchestration, data permissioning, normalization flows

The top-10 fintechs hold 2,400 patents on average.

Your competitors are already filing.
