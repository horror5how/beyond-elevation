AI now writes the first draft of your company valuation.

4 ways investors use it:
- Comparable transaction search (faster, not better)
- Revenue multiple benchmarking (accurate for comps, wrong for moat)
- DCF stress-testing (10x faster than analysts)
- IP and intangible scoring (this is where it breaks)

The problem: AI valuation models are trained on disclosed financial data.

Your IP — patents, data assets, proprietary know-how — lives off-balance-sheet.

AI can't see it.

That's where the $3-8M discount happens in a deal.

The founders who close at premium multiples get an independent IP valuation before the AI model runs.

Not after.
