USPTO ruled: AI cannot be a named inventor. But your patent is still safe.

Here is what most founders miss.

If a human directed the AI, shaped the prompt, and applied the output — that human is the inventor.

The patent is theirs.

Companies inventing with AI and skipping human-in-the-loop documentation are filing unenforceable patents.

USPTO, EPO, and UK IPO all require documented human inventive contribution in 2026.

3 jurisdictions. 1 rule. Document it or lose it.
