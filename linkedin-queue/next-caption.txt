The 30 days after you sign an LOI is when your exit multiple is decided.

Not in negotiations.

Acquirers spend 4 hours reviewing your IP package. Founders who enter due diligence with one active license — even $50K/year — exit at 2.4x more per patent cluster than those without.

Most IP schedules are a list of filing dates. Zero revenue. Zero leverage.

Three documents change the number: an active license agreement, a 24-month FTO opinion, and an IP Revenue Ratio above 8%.

None require filing new applications.
