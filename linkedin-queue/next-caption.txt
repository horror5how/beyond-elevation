Your data is worth $0 on your balance sheet.

Strategic buyers disagree — by up to $180M.

Most founders hire a patent attorney to value their data. That gets you an opinion. Not a number a CFO will underwrite.

Data asset valuation uses three distinct methods: income-based DCF, comparable licensing transactions, replacement cost.

They should not agree. The gap between them is your negotiating range.

Companies exiting at 4x+ revenue multiples have a third-party data appraisal in the data room before the LOI lands.

Most do not.
