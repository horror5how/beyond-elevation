David Bowie raised $55M in 1997 by packaging his song royalties into bonds.

Most founders don't know the same structure now applies to patents.

IP securitisation turns your royalty stream into a note investors can buy.

You get liquidity today. You keep the IP.

The numbers:

→ Minimum viable royalty stream: ~$2M/year
→ Advance rate against NPV: 60–70%
→ Bowie Bond yield at issue: 7.9%

Your patents are generating cash. That cash can be packaged.

Most founders leave this option entirely off the table.
