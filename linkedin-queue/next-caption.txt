Most founders get one wire when they exit.
Smart ones negotiate two.

Wire 1: the acquisition price.

Wire 2: the royalty check that arrives every quarter after you leave.

A product line sells for 4x revenue.
A royalty annuity on that same IP sells for 8-12x income and keeps compounding.

One client retained a patent portfolio in a holdco before closing.
The acquirer got the business.
The founder kept the IP.
$180K/year in licensing. At 10x income multiple, that is $1.8M outside the deal.

Most founders give both away.
