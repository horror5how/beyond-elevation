NDAs do not protect know-how. 5 documents do.

Most founders sign NDAs and assume their IP is safe.

NDAs only cover what you share externally.

They don't cover what employees invent.
They don't cover contractor output.
They don't stop know-how leaving in a laptop bag.

The 5-document stack:

1. IP assignment agreement — every hire, day one
2. NDA — covers external disclosures only
3. Written trade secret policy — defines confidential
4. AI acceptable use policy — blocks the ChatGPT leak
5. Exit protocol — logs what leaves when staff go

48 hours to implement. Zero equity diluted.

One licensing deal pays for the lawyer.
