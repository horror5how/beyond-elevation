Defensibility overtook growth as AI's #1 valuation driver.

81% of Q1 2026 VC went to AI.

40% of seed and Series A rounds are now $100M+ tickets.

Same ARR. Radically different multiples.

The question isn't how fast you're growing.

It's: can a well-funded competitor rebuild this in 18 months?

If yes — your multiple is capped.

If no — you command a 4x premium on comparable revenue.

Three things that set the defensibility score:

Living data moat, not a static dataset.

Patent coverage depth, not provisional filings.

Workflow integration that creates switching cost.

Growth gets you in the room. Defensibility sets the price.
