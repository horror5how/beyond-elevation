Most founders are carrying IP debt. None of them know the number.

Like technical debt, it compounds silently.

Every month you delay filing:
A competitor files first.
VCs haircut your valuation 15-30% at Series B.
Remediation costs rise.

The bill arrives in diligence.

Acquirers subtract IP remediation from your price.
Average: 20-40% of undefended IP value.

3 signs you have IP debt:
1. Innovations in production with no application filed.
2. Engineers who left without IP assignment agreements.
3. AI tools in your R&D with no ownership clause.

IP debt is not a legal problem.

It is a balance sheet problem.
