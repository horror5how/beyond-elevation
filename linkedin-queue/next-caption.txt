Most IP licensing deals fall apart at renewal.

Not because the technology failed. Because the deal was structured wrong.

The metric that predicts renewal: Year 2 licensee revenue growth.

If a licensee grew ≥15% using your IP in Year 2, renewal rate is 91%.

If they grew <5%, renewal rate falls to 34%.

The implication: structure deals with growth tiers, not flat minimums.

Minimum guarantees protect you.

Growth tiers make you rich.
