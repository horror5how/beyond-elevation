You signed that licensing deal 18 months ago. You left 40% of the annual rate on the table.

Three things give you renegotiation leverage your contract almost certainly permits.

Your licensee launched a new product in your field.

The licensed territory expanded.

Your patent portfolio gained a continuation or new grant.

Most founders miss all three windows. The contract runs at the original rate forever.

One letter sent at the right trigger resets the rate. Average uplift: 40%.

On a $300K/year deal, that is $120K added annually. No new legal fees. Same contract.
