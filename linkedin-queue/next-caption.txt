IP indemnification escrow is standard in every M&A deal.

15% of deal value. Locked for 12–18 months.

Released only after no IP claims surface post-close.

Founders celebrate the headline number. Then wait 18 months for the rest.

Three fixes before the LOI eliminate the clause entirely: FTO on core product, signed IP assignments from all employees, documented trade secrets.

Fix IP before you go to market. Not after.
