Ericsson earned €2.6B from patent licensing last year.

Nokia earned €1.5B.

That is 80% of their combined operating profit.

Not from products. From patents declared essential to 5G standards.

Three things about standard essential patents most founders miss:

1. Every manufacturer in the standard MUST license. No opt-out.
2. FRAND terms force royalties to flow whether you are selling or not.
3. One SEP compounds for up to 20 years as the standard spreads.

You do not need to be Ericsson.

A single SEP position in IoT or AI chips rewrites your exit multiple.
