$5M ARR from subscriptions and $5M ARR from data licensing are not the same company.

One trades at 8x.

The other trades at 28x.

AI revenue quality is what separates a lifestyle business from a fundable asset.

3 tiers investors price in 2026:

Tier 1 — Proprietary data licensing: recurring, defensible, 25–30x.

Tier 2 — Usage-based API: scalable but commoditising, 12–18x.

Tier 3 — Seat-based AI tools: replaceable, 6–10x.

If your revenue is Tier 3, your deck is Tier 3.

Fix the revenue model before you fix the pitch.
