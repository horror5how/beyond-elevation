You can license a trade secret.

You can also destroy it with one bad contract.

Most founders don't know the difference.

A patent lasts 20 years. A trade secret lasts forever — until you disclose it.

License wrong and the protection is gone. No appeal. No fix.

The 4 clauses AI labs put in every know-how license:

1. Confidentiality carve-out tied to the license term

2. Strict field-of-use restriction

3. No reverse-engineering obligation on the licensee

4. Return or destroy provision on termination

One missing clause. Zero trade secret protection. Forever.
