Most founders raise 3 rounds. 1 licensing deal would have been cheaper.

License fee: 0% equity diluted.
Royalty stream: 90%+ gross margin.
Exit multiple: 2-4x higher for IP-generating companies.

Most founders only think about licensing after they've raised.
Smart founders use licensing to make the next raise unnecessary.

When to license:
After patent grant: pilot license to one strategic partner.
At Series A: licensing revenue changes your margin profile.
Before exit: recurring royalty income adds 2-4x to acquisition multiple.

You already have IP worth licensing.
You haven't found the buyer yet.
