Your IP is generating £0.

There are 6 routes to fix that.

Licensing: fastest. Sell the right, keep the asset.

Cross-licensing: swap rights with a competitor. Zero cash. High leverage.

Litigation licensing: a well-drafted letter. 80% of cases settle.

IP sale: one-time payout. Best when you own a portfolio.

IP-backed debt: borrow against patents. No dilution.

Spin-out: separate entity, separate valuation. Protects your core multiple.

Most founders pick the wrong route and leave 2x on the table.
