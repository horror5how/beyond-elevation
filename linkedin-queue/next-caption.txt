Your IP is worth 3x more to a strategic acquirer.

Most founders price it for a financial buyer.

Strategic acquirers pay for market control, not cash flow.

If your patent blocks a competitor's product line, it's worth the revenue that line would have generated — not just your licensing income.

Financial buyers price on DCF. Strategic buyers price on threat elimination.

Knowing the difference before you enter the LOI room is worth $5-15M.

Most founders never learn this until the deal is signed.
