Your acquirer runs an IP revenue quality score. Most founders fail it.

Here is what they check:

1. Is licensing revenue documented - or just invoiced?

2. Are rights sublicensable and assignable to the buyer?

3. Is there a minimum guarantee - or pure usage risk?

4. Are royalty rates at or above market benchmark?

Score below 3 of 4 and your 8x multiple becomes 5x overnight.

Most founders only find out after the LOI is signed.

Fix the documentation before you need it.
