Two AI companies. Same agent architecture. Same market.

One raised at 47x revenue. The other at 9x.

The difference: one owned its agent IP. The other didn't.

Agentic AI is not a product decision. It is an IP decision.

Three moves the 47x company made:

1. Patented the orchestration logic, not the model.

2. Registered training pipelines as trade secrets.

3. Structured IP in a holdco before the Series B.

74% of agentic AI startups skip all three.

That is why their valuation looks like a commodity.
