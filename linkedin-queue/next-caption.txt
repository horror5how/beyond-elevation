Founders pay $400/hr for a patent attorney to file patents.

They pay $0/hr for strategy.

A patent attorney answers: "Can we file this?"

A patent strategist answers: "Should we file this, and who will license it?"

One question costs $25K.
The other earns it back in year one.

Companies with a filing strategy exit at 2.1x higher multiples.
Companies that file reflexively own patents nobody licenses.

The switch is not expensive.
It is a different question.
