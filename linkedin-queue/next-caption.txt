Your competitor cut their IP budget 18 months ago.

That decision shows up in your deal comps next quarter.

A 12-patent portfolio costs $180K a year to maintain.

Most CFOs kill it.

18 months later they pay $2.4M to rebuild it. The window to use it in a raise has closed.

Three things to do before you cut:
Audit what is actively generating defensive value
Flag what lapses in the next 12 months
Separate the $12K patents from the $400K ones

The ones worth keeping cost less than a sales hire.
