Most founders used AI tools to draft or refine patent claims after early 2024.

Most did not document AI's role in the invention record.

The USPTO's 2024 guidance is clear: if AI contributed to conception, that must be tracked and disclosed. Inventorship defects do not expire.

They surface in the data room. At the worst possible moment.

Average licensing value at risk per patent family: $3M.
Cost to audit and remediate before diligence: $8K.
Time before IPR or M&A due diligence exposes it: 12–36 months.

Most companies have not run this audit.
