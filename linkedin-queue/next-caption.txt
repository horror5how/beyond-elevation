90% of S&P 500 value is intangible.

Most founders book it at zero.

That gap is what acquirers exploit when pricing your business.

Three balance sheet errors that kill your IP valuation:

Expensing R&D instead of capitalising it.

Booking patents at filing cost, not income value.

Treating proprietary data as overhead, not an asset.

Fix all three before your next raise.
