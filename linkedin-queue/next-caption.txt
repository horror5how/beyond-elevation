VCs don't fund your AI roadmap. They fund your moat.

Most founders pitch capabilities. Investors run a 5-axis defensibility check.

1. Proprietary data — can it be replicated?
2. Patent density — claims on core methods?
3. Switching cost — how expensive to replace you?
4. Network effects — does value compound?
5. Regulatory moat — is your IP a compliance shortcut?

Score under 3 of 5 → commodity pricing. Score 4–5 → platform multiple.

IP-protected AI companies trade at 5x the multiple of unprotected peers.

Run the score before your next raise.
