A $3,000 patent protects what a $50,000 patent cannot.

Utility patents protect how your product works.

Design patents protect how it looks.

Apple's rounded-rectangle design patent earned them $539M from Samsung. One filing.

Cost to file a US design patent: $1,500–$3,000.

Time to grant: 12–18 months. Utility patents take 3–4 years.

Enforceable term: 15 years.

Most hardware and product founders file one utility patent and ignore the appearance entirely.

Then a competitor copies the UI, the form factor, the packaging.

And you have no recourse. You never protected the look.

File the design patent. Costs less than one lawyer consultation.
