We ran IP audits on 40 companies.

Every single one had assets they didn't know they owned.

The 3 that show up every time:

Undocumented know-how locked in senior engineers' heads.

Software workflows that qualify for trade secret protection but were never filed.

Lapsed provisional patents still within the re-filing window.

EUIPO data: SMEs that register and document IP see a 65% valuation uplift.

Most founders don't fail because they lack IP.

They fail because they never looked.
