Two AI companies. Same ARR. One valued at 8x. One at 80x.

The investor ran 6 numbers.

Data exclusivity — proprietary datasets command 30x. Scraped data gets 8x.

IP defensibility — patents, trade secrets, licensing potential scored to 100.

Model differentiation — fine-tuned architecture beats wrapper every time.

Workflow stickiness — embedded in daily ops vs bolt-on tool.

Intangible asset share — IP + data + know-how as % of total valuation.

Most founders track ARR. They ignore the 5 numbers driving 80% of the multiple.

That's how $50M+ gets left on the table at exit.
