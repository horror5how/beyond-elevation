July is when founders review revenue and ignore IP.

3 numbers to know before August:

1. Maintenance fees due before December
Miss the window and the patent lapses. Most founders don't know their renewal schedule.

2. Active licensing renewal clauses
Most agreements have a 90-day notice window. August to October is peak renewal season. Miss it, restart from zero.

3. Unlicensed value sitting dormant
This is the number VCs calculate in due diligence. Know it before they do.

Three numbers. One audit. Run it now.
