5 AI companies built moats nobody can copy.

They didn't do it by shipping features first.

They did it by filing before anyone else knew what to file.

Qualcomm: 7,500+ patents on wireless tech. Every competitor pays a toll.

OpenAI: exclusive MSFT compute deal + proprietary RLHF stack as a trade secret. No 20-year clock.

Apple: 200+ Face ID algorithm patents. Every Android phone maker pays into it.

DeepMind: AlphaFold patents filed before the model was public. The science is free. The commercial use is not.

The common thread: IP before distribution. Not after.

By the time your users show up, your moat should already be filed, registered, or locked.
