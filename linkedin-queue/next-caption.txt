Your pitch deck has revenue. Headcount. Burn rate.

It doesn't have these 3.

1. Total addressable licensing market — how big the royalty pool is in your sector

2. IP monetisation rate — what % of your portfolio is generating income vs sitting dormant

3. Implied royalty ARR — the recurring revenue your IP could show at Series B pricing

VCs at growth stage now run a parallel IP diligence track.

Founders who show these 3 numbers close at 23% higher pre-money valuations in IP-intensive sectors.

Most founders learn this number exists at due diligence.

By then it is too late to change it.
