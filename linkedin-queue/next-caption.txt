PE acquirers apply a 35–45% valuation haircut to AI companies with undocumented IP.

Most founders discover this during due diligence. By then, the multiple is set.

The fix is three things: a clean IP ownership chain, 3+ patent claims filed, and a documented data licensing agreement.

Cost of getting this right before a deal: under $100K in fractional IP strategy.

Cost of skipping it: the haircut.

Document before you raise. Not after.
