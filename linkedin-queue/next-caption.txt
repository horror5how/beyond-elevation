Most founders name a patent value. None of them calculated it.

4 methods exist. Each returns a radically different number.

Cost approach — what you spent to build it. Lowest number.

Income/DCF — future royalties, discounted. What courts and acquirers use.

Real options — your patent as a call on future market size. Highest number.

The spread between cost and income on a single patent: up to 40x.

Know which model your acquirer will run before the LOI arrives.
