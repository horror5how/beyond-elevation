Two AI companies. Same revenue. One raised at 8x. The other at 80x.

The difference isn't the product. It's 4 factors investors actually price:

IP defensibility. Patents, trade secrets, model weights. The moat, not the model.

Data exclusivity. Proprietary training data commands a premium. Scraped data is priced like a commodity.

Revenue quality. ARR with 130%+ NRR trades at 2x the multiple of churn-heavy contracts.

Talent lock-in. Key-person dependency without IP assignment kills valuation in due diligence.

Most founders pitch features. Investors price moat architecture.

Fix the 4 factors before the roadshow.
