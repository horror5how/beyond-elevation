Most founders license their IP once. The smart ones license it five ways simultaneously.

They have one patent. One licensee. One revenue stream.

The same IP supports 5 streams at once.

Geographic: UK vs US vs APAC = 3 separate deals.

Field of use: healthcare vs fintech vs logistics. Same patent. Three rates.

Know-how: the methodology is its own license.

Data: your training set is a separate asset.

Cross-license: swap rights with a competitor for free moat expansion.

90% gross margin. Zero dilution.

Your patent isn't one deal. It's a revenue architecture.
