Series B investors model your IP for risk.

Series C investors model it for revenue.

Most founders pitch the same deck to both.

That's why 40% of AI startups fail Series C IP due diligence.

At B: prove you won't get sued.

At C: prove you can license your way to an 8x multiple.

3 moves to make between rounds:
- File continuations to lock claim breadth
- Sign your first licensing deal — even a small one proves the model
- Get a formal IP valuation on the balance sheet

By Series C, your IP should be generating revenue. Not just protecting it.
