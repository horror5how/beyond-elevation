You can license your trade secret for recurring revenue.

One missing clause destroys it permanently.

A trade secret only exists while it stays secret.

Disclose it without guardrails and courts will rule it public domain.

The 4 clauses AI labs use to protect trade secrets through licensing:

Residuals bar.

Destruction clause.

Indefinite confidentiality obligation.

No sublicense provision.

Miss any one and your algorithm, your training data, your know-how becomes worthless overnight.

Most founders miss all four.
