Coca-Cola's formula has been a trade secret for 137 years.

A patent would have expired in 1903.

Most founders default to patents. Trade secrets often protect moats longer.

Patents expire in 20 years. Trade secrets never expire.

Patents publish your method to every competitor. Trade secrets keep it hidden.

Patents cost £15K+ to file and renew. Trade secret registration costs £0.

The catch: once it leaks, you cannot enforce it. No proof, no case.

Know which tool fits your moat before you file.
