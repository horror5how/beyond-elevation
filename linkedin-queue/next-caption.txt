Flat royalty rates leave 2x on the table.

Every licensing deal has 3 value layers.

Layer 1: Upfront fee — compensates for exclusivity and integration costs.
Layer 2: Running royalty — 3–7% net sales, 8–12% for software top quartile.
Layer 3: Revenue milestones — unlocked at deal thresholds, not time.

Companies using all three collect 2–3x the lifetime deal value of flat-rate structures.

Most founders negotiate the rate.
The smart ones negotiate the structure.
