Your patent attorney wrote your claims narrow. That decision costs you 3x in licensing revenue.

Patent claim breadth determines how many companies can infringe.

Narrow claims = one exact product to target.

Broad claims = 47 potential licensees in the same market.

The revenue gap between broad and narrow claims: $2.1M per portfolio.

Your attorney optimised for grant rate. You should optimise for revenue.

Claim scope is a business decision. Not a legal one.
