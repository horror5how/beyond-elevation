5 data licensing models exist.

Most founders are using the worst one.

Subscription: capped upside, the buyer wins.

Per-query: scales with their usage, you win.

Revenue-share: your dataset becomes a royalty line.

Exclusive deals command a 3-5x premium over non-exclusive.

Tiered access creates upsells your CFO isn't modelling.

Top AI firms earn 11% of revenue from data assets. The rest earn 2%.

The gap is the model you choose.
