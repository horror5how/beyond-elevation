The M&A buyer paid 4.8x. I spent a week figuring out why.

It was not the product. It was not the team. It was not the ARR.

IP with a licensing history trades at 8x revenue at exit.

IP with no licensing history gets valued at cost. Usually $0.

Three things that move the IP price in a deal: licensed, documented, clean chain of title.

Most founders spend 3 years building a patent portfolio. Zero days building the licensing paper trail that makes it worth $4M to a buyer.

Fix that before the data room opens.
