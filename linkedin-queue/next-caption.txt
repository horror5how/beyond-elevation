August 2 is 17 days away.

Every AI company deploying a general-purpose AI model needs documented training data lineage by then.

Most don't have it.

The GPAI fine: €35M or 7% of global revenue.

Three documents protect you:

A training data inventory listing every dataset, its source, date, and rights status.

Licensing or fair-use documentation for every third-party dataset in your pipeline.

Technical records that trace model outputs back to training inputs.

One licensing deal signed now costs less than one week of remediation later.
