Most founders take the lump sum. That's usually wrong.

Your licensee offers $500K upfront or 5% of their revenue for 10 years.

You take the lump sum.

They generate $4.6M from your IP over the decade.

Running royalty: $2.3M.
Lump sum: $500K.

You left $1.8M on the table. One negotiation. One choice.

Running royalties compound. Lump sums don't.

Before you structure the next deal, run the model.
