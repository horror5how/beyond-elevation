A $5M royalty line in your S-1 is worth more than $20M in product revenue.

Royalty income has no COGS. Product revenue does.

Qualcomm's licensing segment earns 70% EBIT margin. Its chip segment earns 20%.

Bankers price royalty revenue at 8–12x. SaaS at 3–5x. Same dollar, different multiple.

Most pre-IPO companies have unlicensed patents generating $0.

Legal owns them. Finance ignores them.

The window to add a royalty line before your S-1: 18–24 months minimum. Not 90 days.

If you're planning an IPO in the next 3 years, the IP audit should start now.

Most founders find this out after the S-1 is filed.
