Most founders have no idea what their IP is worth.

Their investor does. And it's probably lower than reality.

3 methods that set the real number:

Income approach: future royalty cash flows, discounted back to today.

Market approach: comparable transactions. Median patent family deal: $300K-$2M.

Cost approach: what it cost to build the asset, adjusted for obsolescence.

A licensed patent family typically returns 3-8x the filing cost.

Run the valuation before they do.
