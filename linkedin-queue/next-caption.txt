4 patent due diligence items kill 30% of M&A deals.

Acquirers run a 14-point checklist.

Most founders see it for the first time after signing the LOI.

The 4 that collapse deals:

1. FTO gap — your patent exists. It doesn't cover the product you ship.

2. Broken inventor chain — one ex-employee never signed the assignment.

3. Lapsed maintenance — a patent in your deck expired silently.

4. Encumbered IP — you licensed rights you don't control to a competitor.

Fix these before the data room opens.
