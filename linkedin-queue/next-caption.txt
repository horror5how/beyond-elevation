Two AI startups. Same ARR. One valuation is 40% higher.

The difference is not the product.

Not the team.

Not the growth rate.

It is the IP defensibility score investors run before they wire.

Replication speed. Data moat depth. Patent coverage. Licensing optionality.

Score 4+ on these axes: you close at 8x forward revenue.

Score 2 or below: you close at 5-6x. Or you do not close.

Your ARR sets the floor. Your defensibility sets the ceiling.
