Most founders price a patent licence by guessing.

The median software royalty rate is 8–12% of net sales.

SaaS and pharma benchmark: 15%+.

Electronics: 4–6%.

If you are charging a flat fee with no escalation clause, you are leaving compounding revenue on the table.

The gap between a 3% rate and an 8% rate on £5M revenue is £250K per year — every year.

Royalty rate is a financial decision. Not a legal one.

Know your benchmark before you walk into any licensing room.
