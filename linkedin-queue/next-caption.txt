40% of your IP value is invisible to investors. Because nobody put it in the model.

Patent royalties don't appear in most startup financial forecasts.

So acquirers discount. VCs haircut. Buyers assume the IP is worthless.

Three numbers fix this:
Addressable licensed use (TAM of potential licensees)
Industry royalty rate (software 3–8%, biotech 6–12%)
Minimum guarantee term (lock in 3–5 year floors)

Add a licensing line to your next board deck. Separate from product ARR. Watch the conversation change.
