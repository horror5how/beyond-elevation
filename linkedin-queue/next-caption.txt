IBM filed 9,100 patents last year. More than half were continuations.

Most founders file one patent and stop.

Continuation filing lets you keep refining claims from the same priority date — as your product evolves.

The result: a patent fortress that compounds over time, not a single wall competitors route around.

3 moves that build it:

File the provisional.

File the utility.

File the continuation the day you ship version 2.

Most founders skip step 3. That is why their IP protection collapses in year 4.
