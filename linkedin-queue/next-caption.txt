Your best engineer just quit. Who actually owns what they built?

If you didn't get a signed IP assignment at hire, the answer might not be you.

Most startups assume employment = IP ownership. UK and US courts disagree.

3 things a proper IP assignment covers:
— Work done on company time and equipment
— Code written on personal devices after hours
— Side projects that overlap with your product

Miss one and a £20M acquisition falls apart at legal.

Fix this before you raise. Not after.
