Most AI founders are booking AI costs to COGS. That one decision is cutting their exit multiple by 30%.

AI inference booked to COGS: gross margin drops 18 points. Revenue multiple compresses to 4x.

Same spend. Classified as R&D: gross margin stays intact. Multiple holds at 8x.

On $25M ARR that difference is $100M in exit value before negotiation even starts.

GAAP, IFRS, and the IRS all have tests for when AI infrastructure qualifies as R&D. None of your investors will flag the wrong classification before term sheet.

Your CFO should run this audit before the next raise, not after.
