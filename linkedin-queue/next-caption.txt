AI companies that license model weights raise at 8x revenue multiples.

API wrappers top out at 4–5x.

The difference is the IP layer.

Three moves that change the structure:

1. Document training provenance before filing.
2. Separate base model IP from the fine-tuned layer.
3. License each layer independently on a royalty basis.

Most founders collapse all three into one product line.

That single mistake costs 3x at exit.
