Investors use a rule they never publish.

The 30% rule.

30% of an AI company's valuation premium comes from IP and data defensibility. Not model quality.

Generic AI wrapper: no premium. Valued like SaaS.

Proprietary training data: 12-18% uplift.

Patented architecture plus data flywheel: 28-34% uplift.

All three in an IP holdco: 30% or more above sector median on exit.

Companies raising at 40-80x are not smarter. They are better structured.
